Lean Canvas, super quick!

Easily create a Lean Canvas document in PDF using only your browser.

Based on Business Model Canvas. Optimized for Lean Startup by LeanCanvas.com.
Shared with CC BY SA 3.0. Full source code available with MIT license.

Get started now

Easy

You don't need to join any service, pay any fee or share your data with anybody. The app stores data in your browser storage, so you won't loose your work, if you need to close the window between edits.

Discreet

You don't need to share your great business idea with no-one. The app runs entirely on your browser as a Angular.js app, PDF export is included thanks to the JsPDF library

Open-source

You can fork the app on GitHub and edit it to suit your needs. Full source included
